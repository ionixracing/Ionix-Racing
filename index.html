What is STEM Racing?

Established in 1999, STEM Racing is an international competition that enables young engineers, aged 9 to 19, to compete in order to achieve the final goal of building the best miniature racecar. STEM Racing challenges its participants to combine numerous facets of engineering, including manufacturing skills, business management, analytical skills, coding skills, and collaborative skills.
